How We Work

We make angel-sized investments. While we have First Round's financial and operational support, we invest and operate independently.

We're targeting founders who've had a first job or two, recently out of undergrad or grad school, thinking about starting their first company. If you're looking for a partner to guide you through the fund raising processs or bring product expertise to your company, we may be a good fit.

Frequently asked questions

What value does Graduate Fund offer to founders?

We are early founders and product managers at successful tech companies. We strive to make raising from us simple and as low friction as possible. As a group, we know how to raise early funding, have a strong network in the Valley and can help you think about your business. We're in your shoes, building products day-to-day, raising rounds and hiring. We're more than happy to share our learnings and help you with the challenges your startup faces.

When and how much does Graduate Fund invest in a company?

We invest up to $50k in startups at seed stage and earlier.

Do you invest outside of the Bay Area?

Yes. While we're based in San Francisco, we invest in startups across the country.

Where does your funding come from? Do you invest your own capital?

Graduate Fund is backed by First Round. Partners are open to independently investing as well.

How can I get in touch with Graduate Fund?

The best way to start a conversation with us is to reach out to one of us through your network. If we don’t have mutual connection, you can email us directly at talktous@graduatefund.com, and we'll do our best to respond.
